GoPlayMM is a free, open-source alternative to Valve's Counter-Strike: Global Offensive Matchmaking service. It features fast, high-performance 128-tick servers, a simple web-interface, extensive stats, competitive rule-set, anticheat, and more!

GoPlayMM strives to provide the honest, fun, and reliable gaming experience CS:GO players deserve. We have a no-bullshit policy, and believe in clearly explaining the facts rather than hiding behind veils of lies. We're proud our entire playform is completely Open Source. Together with players, we hope to help grow and nuture the CS:GO gaming community. GoPlayMM is 100% free to use, and even if you choose to donate, you won't get any super-special treatment (just a few cool features!).

Beta

Features

  • High-Performance, Consistent, Cross-Region 128tick servers

  • Light-weight and simple web-based Lobby/Queue System

  • No custom client; use vanilla Steam/CS:GO

  • Extensive list of Valve and Community maps to queue for

  • Statistics, Achievements, 64-tick GOTV demos with voice

  • Intelligent, skill-based ranking algorithim (e.g. No ELO)

  • Multi-factor cheat/grief detection and prevention system

  • And a ton more!
