About GoPlayMM

GoPlayMM is a free, open-source and kind of kick-ass matchmaking for Counter Strike: Global Offensive. It was built in the hopes it can reduce the frustrations and short-comings that Valve's matchmaking service can induce. GoPlayMM aims to provide consistent 128tick servers, an easy to use platform, additional maps, and in-depth stats to users, which enhance and build upon Valve's matchmaking concepts.
